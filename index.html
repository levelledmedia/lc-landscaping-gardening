Our Services

Mowing and Strimming

Complete mowing and strimming to keep your lawn healthy and well-maintained throughout the season. Flexible scheduling options available for regular or one-off visits.

Hedge and Shrub Cutting

Professional cutting of hedges and shrubs to enhance the appearance of your garden. Regular maintenance options ensure your plants remain healthy and vibrant.

Borders and Flower Beds

Design and maintenance of borders and flower beds to create beautiful and inviting outdoor spaces. Tailored services available to suit your garden's specific needs.

Turfing

Installation of high-quality turf to create a lush, green lawn instantly. Expert advice on lawn care and maintenance provided to ensure longevity.

Fencing

Professional installation and repair of fencing to enhance privacy and security in your garden. Various styles and materials available to match your preferences.

Patios and Light Brickwork

Construction of patios and light brickwork to improve your outdoor living space. Custom designs available to fit your style and functionality needs.

Block Paving

Expert block paving services for driveways and pathways to enhance your property's curb appeal. Durable materials ensure long-lasting results.

Repointing

Careful removal and replacement of worn or damaged mortar to restore strength and improve the appearance of your brickwork. Suitable for both full and partial repointing, with flexible options for one-off or scheduled maintenance.

Jet Washing

High-pressure jet washing to clean patios, driveways, and garden furniture effectively. Seasonal cleaning options available to keep your outdoor spaces pristine.

Regular Visits

Scheduled landscaping visits to keep your outdoor space tidy, healthy, and looking its best all year round. Flexible plans available to suit your needs, from routine maintenance to seasonal care.

One Offs

One-off landscaping visits for quick tidy-ups, seasonal clearances, or specific jobs when you need them. Flexible and convenient service tailored to your requirements, with no ongoing commitment.

Garden Waste Clearance

Efficient garden waste clearance to keep your outdoor space tidy and clutter-free. Regular visits or one-off services available to suit your schedule.

Fence Painting and Repair

Professional fence painting and repair services to maintain and enhance the appearance of your fencing. Regular maintenance options available for longevity.

Repair Work

Reliable repair work to fix damaged or worn features and restore your outdoor space to its best condition. Suitable for a range of landscaping issues, with flexible options for one-off jobs or as part of ongoing maintenance.

Artificial Grass

Professional installation of artificial grass to create a neat, low-maintenance lawn that looks great all year round. Suitable for a range of outdoor spaces, with flexible options for full supply and fit or installation only.

Concrete Sub Bases

Professional preparation and installation of solid concrete sub bases for sheds, summer houses, greenhouses, and outdoor structures. Built to provide a durable, level foundation that stands the test of time.

Sheds

Supply and installation of garden sheds tailored to your outdoor space. From compact storage solutions to larger workshop-style sheds, all builds are completed with care and precision.

Summer Houses

Custom summer house assembly and installation to create the perfect outdoor retreat. Ideal for relaxing, entertaining, or adding extra usable space to your garden.

Greenhouses

Expert greenhouse installation services designed to give your plants a secure and stable growing environment. Careful assembly ensures strength, ventilation, and long-lasting performance.

Rebuild and Dismantle Services

Safe dismantling, relocation, and rebuilding of sheds, summer houses, and garden structures. Perfect for replacing old units, moving home, or refreshing your outdoor setup.

Decking Installations

High-quality timber decking installations designed to enhance your garden and outdoor living area. Finished to a professional standard with attention to both appearance and durability.

Decking Staining

Protect and refresh your decking with professional staining treatments. Helps preserve the timber against weather damage while improving the overall look of your outdoor space.

Raised Garden Planter Beds

Bespoke raised planter beds built to suit any garden style or size. Great for flowers, vegetables, and easy-maintenance gardening with a neat, attractive finish.

Sleepers

Timber sleeper installations for borders, retaining walls, steps, and decorative garden features. A practical and stylish way to add structure and definition to outdoor spaces.

Gravel Pathways

Installation of durable gravel pathways that provide both practicality and visual appeal. A cost-effective solution for gardens, driveways, and access routes with a clean finished look.

Decorative Pathways

Beautifully designed decorative pathways created to complement your garden and landscape. Available in a range of styles and materials to add character and functionality to your outdoor areas.
